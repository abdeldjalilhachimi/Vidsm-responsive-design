How to download video online?

Copy your video URL from social media video that you need to download. You can use Ctrl+C or with the context menu

Paste YouTube video URL in the search field . Use Ctrl+V

You will get a list of files for download, video, and audio. Click the 'Download' button to start downloading the file.
